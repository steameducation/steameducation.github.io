Steam Education - Recipes for Creative Classrooms

As the final event of the Erasmus Plus SPACE project, Curso de Musica Silva Monteiro invite you to join them in Porto for a one day symposium to explore the resources and learning created through the project by the partners.

9am - 6pm Saturday 30th March 2019

The day will explore how to use creative techniques and arts to deliver science outcomes in the classroom. Content will include a keynote speaker (Rosa Dora, NUCLIO), STEAM recipes, presentations of the resources created through the SPACE project, presentations of other portuguese STEAM projects, a round table discussion and a concert.

Registration is free but you need to register by filling in the BOOKING FORM
