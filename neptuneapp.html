SPACE Resource - Neptune App

As part of our project, we worked with students from Ireland, Norway and Belgium to develop a STEAM app for use in the classroom.

We created an app that lets you explore the planets in our solarsystem whilst also making music, exploring sounds from space, or uploading your own sounds. We hope you enjoy using it with your pupils. You can explore it right here: http://neptune.nunoh.com/.

You can follow us on facebook and twitter, where we are already sharing resources and articles that we like.
