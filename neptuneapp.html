SPACE Resource - Neptune App

As part of our project, we worked with students from Ireland, Norway and Belgium to develop a STEAM app for use in the classroom.

We created an app, with the help of our partners at Digitópia Casa da Música, that lets you explore the planets in our solar system whilst also making music, exploring sounds from space, or uploading your own sounds. We hope you enjoy using it with your pupils.

You can explore it right here: Neptune App

From November 13th 2019, the app will be available at http://steameducation.eu/neptune/. Some downtime is to be expected during that day.
