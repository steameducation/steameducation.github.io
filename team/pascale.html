Pascale Mast - Lecturer in Education

Pascale graduated in 1989 as an industrial engineering engineer, worked for 8 years at a construction company and since 1997 has been affiliated with the former autonomous Antwerp University College (now AP) as a construction, timber and engineering lecturer in the professional secondary education bachelor. Supervisor of internships and bachelor's theses. Co-researcher in the PWO project 'Discover Castle', the PWO project 'Science and technology for primary education including transfer to 1st grade secondary education', the PWO project 'Co-Prof in STEM' and 'STEM'ige instruments' and the 'Diversity in Science Communication' research project. Project manager in the Erasmus + project 'Space'. For five years she has been an employee on the 'Technogirls' and 'Technoteens' projects in collaboration with Agoria.
