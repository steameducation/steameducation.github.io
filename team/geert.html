Geert Marrin - Lecturer in Education

Geert Marrin is the initiator and coordinator of the strategic partnership SPACE. He is a policy advisor within the international office of AP University of Applied Sciences and Arts Antwerp and has a multidisciplinary study and professional background in journalism, communication, political policy work, social work and teacher training. At AP University of Applied Sciences and Arts Antwerp, he provides advice and support in the field of internationalization of curricula, international and interdisciplinary project work and development cooperation.
