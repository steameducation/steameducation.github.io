Dr Ronan Lynch - Lecturer in Creative Arts, Media and Music

Dr Ronan Lynch is a Lecturer in Dundalk Institute of Technology whose primary areas of interest include Web Development and The Creative Industries. His Phd examined Alternate Reality Games and their educational value. He engages in the creation, design, and development of graphic, audio-visual or web-based projects from idea generation through to the final product.
