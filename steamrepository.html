SPACE Resource - Steam Recipe Repository

As part of our project, we worked with students from Ireland, Norway and Belgium to develop STEAM resources for teachers and workshop leaders. We hope you enjoy the resources we have developed.

We have created a STEAM Recipe Online Repository where we will also be featuring STEAM Recipes, and where you can also upload your own recipes to share.

You can explore it right here: STEAM RECIPES

To give you a taste of what the Online Repository might contain, we've added some of the Recipes developed through the project below.

STEAM RECIPES - click on the titles to download a PDF of each recipe.

Title Description
  • Light Painting
    		•  This recipe explores shutter speed of a camera and guides pupils to paint with light.
  • Functions and Frequencies
    		•  This recipe shows pupils how to work with function and explores frequencies through music.
  • The Sound in Wind Instruments
    		•  This recipe helps pupils explore waves and how sound travels in waves.
  • Phase Transitions
    		•  This recipe explores solids, liquids and gases and the transitions between them.
  • Fibonacci Numbers in a Poem
    		•  This recipe explore the Fibonacci numbers through poetry.
  • Music with Trash
    		•  This recipe explores global warming and plastic waste through music making.
  • Decoding DaVinci
    		•  This recipe explores the work of Leonardo DaVinci and how to use art to create useful inventions.
  • Magnetism
    		•  This recipe explores how magnets work and uses them to create art.
  • Pollution of the Air
    		•  This recipe explores air pollution and what we can do to solve it.
  • Watercycle
    		•  This recipe explores the water cycle, evaporation and condensation.
