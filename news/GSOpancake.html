How to Make Pancakes with Zero Gravity: a Science Opera

During the international STEAM-week (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts and Mathematics), from 21st till the 24th of November 2017, at AP University College in Antwerp Belgian and international students wrote and performed a Science Opera in context of the worldwide initiative a Global Science Opera “Moon Village”.
Through a WASO-workshop(Write a Science Opera) led by two professors of the Western Norway University of Applied Sciences the students performed a science opera on how to make pancakes on the moon.

GSO is the first initiative to create, produce and perform opera as a global community. The GSO “Moon Village” was created by school institutions in 27 countries.
