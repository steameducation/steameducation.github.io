SPACE project Steam week in Norway

In February 2018 students from Belgium, Ireland and Norway spent a week in Lervick in Norway exploring STEAM education techniques as part of the SPACE Erasmus Plus project.

A range of different experiences took place as inspiration across the week, including creating music with sound apps, exploring space travel through art, a visit from a local storyteller, and a tin smelting workshop.
