SPACE project Steam week in Porto March 2019

In March 2019 students from Belgium, Ireland and Norway came together for our final SPACE Erasmus Plus project Intenstive Programme week in the beautiful city of Porto.

The main business of the week was to prepare for the final SPACE symposium which took place on the final Saturday of the Intensive Programme week. Students also took part in sound workshops at Casa da Musica, visited the Planetarium, heard all about solar eclipses from the team at NUCLIO, and then delivered STEAM workshops to local schools.
