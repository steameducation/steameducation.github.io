A Creative Educational Journey

Opening this guidebook will start a creative educational journey!

Write a Science Opera (WASO) is a transdisciplinary, inquiry-based approach to teaching at the intersection of art and science in schools. In this guidebook teachers will find the reasons and advantages for introducing young pupils to WASO. It provides step-by-step instructions on how to implement WASO in the classroom with both pupils of different levels and abilities.

It is challenging, but the rewards are endless, robust and powerful. No two WASO projects are ever the same! In WASO, teachers hand over control to their pupils. Now it is those pupils’ curiosity and creative imagination which (in dialogue with their teacher) will develop their science and art curriculum. Get ready for an exciting journey…
